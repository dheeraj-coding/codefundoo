What it does

Torrid makes it easy to get smart updates and assistance before and during heatwaves

Built on Android

Supporting android, the most popular mobile OS platform in existence, ensures more people can benefit out of the service and keep themselves safe

Built with privacy in mind

Background services and smart tracking features are fully user configurable, which means Torrid only uses data you are willing to provide

Smart predictions, simple interface

The Torrid server monitors weather data and predicts the possibility of a heatwave in the next few days. Torrid presents this in a simple, intuitive interface, so you don't have to be a tech guru to benefit from Torrid.

Hospital Dashboard

The Torrid hospital dashboard helps hospitals identify torrid users in distress during a heatwave and better allocate resoures to help save more lives

Live heart rate tracking

Torrid can monitor the heart rate provided by a fitness tracker in real time and notify the user of signs of heat strokes, heart attacks and general heat stress during a heatwave

Efficient on the network and on the battery

The Torrid android app is designed to work with the battery management features of Android, so Torrid won't make you run in search of a phone charger.
Torrid also uses very little data, so it should work efficiently on metered and slow network connections

Why heatwaves?

Because heatwaves are deadly. More deadly than you think.

Death toll

Heatwaves often have a death toll in the thousands. The Indian heatwave in 2015 killed over 2500 people and the 2018 Japan heatwave resulted in the hospitalization of 22,000 people and the death of almost 80

Global warming and Climate change

Heatwaves are only going to become more common due the effects of global warming and climate change. The frequency of deadly heatwaves is expected to increase not only in the tropics, but all over the world.

Lack of awareness

Heatwaves are silent killers. One of the reason they are so deadly is because people are less likely to take abnormally hot weather seriously. Hence, this needs to be addressed by highlighting the dangers of heatwaves

Widespread effects

The effects of heatwaves can span across multiple districts or states. Deadly heatwaves are not only limited to the tropics, but can occur in colder climates as well
