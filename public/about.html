Torrid

Torrid is a smart automated system built to help the challenges faced during heat waves. It starts of as a simple detector model which uses temperature records and future estimates to detect heat wave conditions in a region. Every day gives it more data though, and as the database of various parameters is automatically collected and maintained, Machine Learning techniques are used to build a predictor model which can predict heat waves in advance for a given region. This smart data collection and analysis is sure to help gain new insights on heat waves.

If a heat wave is detected, everyone who has the Torrid app in the region is alerted, and the app offers easy to access survival tips to the user. For those with smartwatches, Torrid monitors their heart beat rates during heat waves to detect signs of health issues that might be manifesting in the individual. If detected, it notifies nearby hospitals of their location automatically. Hospitals can register and get information from a dashboard available on the Torrid website.
